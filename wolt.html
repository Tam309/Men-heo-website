Wolt is a technology company with a platform that allows:

  • Customers to order whatever they need on one app
  • Merchants to make additional sales
  • Couriers to make meaningful earnings flexibly

Wolt' primary customers are urban consumers who:

  • Prefer food delivery to their homes
  • Utilize courier services
  • Purchase groceries through the Wolt platform or Wolt Market
  • Merchants are also Wolt's customer in the sense that they pay fee to list their products on the platform and promote their restaurants.

Intermediaries:

  • Wolt's couriers with their blue bags, iconic representatives of the brand, assisting in the delivery of orders to customers
  • The company promotes its services on various platforms like YouTube Ads, Facebook Ads, and Instagram Ads
  • For secure processing of customer payments, Wolt partners with Mobile Pay, Apple Pay, Edenred, Klarna.

Competitors:

  • Foodora, Bolt Food, and Uber Food are Wolt's competitors
  • In particular, Foodora directly competes with Wolt in Finland

Supplier:

  • More than 120,000 merchants and retailers that partner with the company to provide goods and services to its customers.

Publics:

  • Investor such as ICONIQ Growth, Tiger Global, 83North, Highland Europe,...
  • The company must maintain a positive reputation and address the concerns of these stakeholders in order to operate effectively
